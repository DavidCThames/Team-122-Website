Our Team

By reaching out to different schools across the peninsula, we have nearly doubled our previous membership, with 35 members and 10 mentors this year. We have members from York High School, Tabb High School, Denbigh High School, Poquoson High School, Jamestown High School, Peninsula Catholic, etc…. and many homeschooled students. Some dedicated team members even drive as far as half an hour from their home schools to attend meetings. Our team is a community that fosters communication, cooperation, as well as technical skills that students can apply.
